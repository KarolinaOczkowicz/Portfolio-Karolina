UX ACTIVITIES AND THE CATEGORIES OF UCD I APPLY AT MY WORK.

User-centered design (UCD) is an iterative design process in which as a designer I focus on the users and their needs, motivations and pain points in each phase of the design process.

I involve users throughout the design process via a variety of research and design techniques, to create highly usable and accessible products for them.

I strongly believe that data gathered during the research phase and the users´ feedback are very important throughout the process
