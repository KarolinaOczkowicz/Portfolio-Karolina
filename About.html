Karolina Oczkowicz is a UX Designer based in Barcelona.

Hi, I’m Karolina, a freelance designer focused on digital product design. I collaborate with passionate people and companies to create products and services that have an exceptional user experience. Having experience as an in-house employee and working with clients and conducting market research, I've learned the power of listening which today helps me to understand the needs and requirements of users. Optimizing sales processes and taking care of different stages of the sales cycle was a part of my job and gave me a solid foundation and awareness of process which I apply to the UCD process creating products that bring value to people’s lives. I believe that equitable access to information and technology creates a better world and equal opportunities. Aside from all this, I travel (42 countries and still counting), I hike and watch series. I´m based in Barcelona, Spain but originally I´m from the tiny town of the subcarpathian region in Poland.
